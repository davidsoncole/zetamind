Standard Business Partnership Categories

Zetamind provides the following standard partnership categories

Referral

Referral Partners are typically consulting or technology based companies which understand the benefits our solution can bring to their customers or other companies within their network, thus recommending it to them. When opportunities are identified, Referral Partners contact Zetamind Customer Service team, which will then conduct the next steps in the sales process. Referral Partners earn referral fees for sales that result from any new lead they generate.

Strategic Alliance

These partners are characterized as leaders across technical, marketing, sales, or services capabilities and typically represent a strong brand in their industry, specialty or vertical market.

OEM (Original Equipment Manufacturers)

We are in partnership with the OEM like OEM like Microsoft, Google, Zoho and currently discussion with other with the benefit of serving our clients better.

Sales Commission Agents

individual independent sales reps are not employees; they are independent contractors paid on commission to represent our brand in a certain territory.
